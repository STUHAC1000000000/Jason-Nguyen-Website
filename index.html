Jason Nguyen

Theatre, Film, and Digital Production

Greetings, my name is Jason Nguyen. Current student but aspiring to become an established worker in this large industry system that is entertainment. I’ve had a passion for the medium, my exposure to the medium is what helped me start and end sentences correctly back in Kindergarten. I start generally, get to something, end it on a point to leave an impression; little did I know that it was part of a formula the well known screenwriter John Truby formulated among his books. The terminology that was used on this writing structure was called “The Last Line was the Point of the Scene”. That's another thing getting me wanting to pursue film, curiosity of how these “hidden” rules, “ways”, and structures in all aspects of media can create something so impressionable.

With education and guidance, I have acquired many skills that show my potential as an animator and storyteller. While I am currently a junior in college, I have developed several projects proven to be worthy of interest to those looking for new life and talent in the arts. Gaining several awards such as the Prestigious Art Award from ArtPrize, and the Bucksbaum Award: all demonstrating high regard from many professionals, understanding of culture appeal, and skills that many other artists’ lack. As for my traits, I have learned to be responsible for my actions, to always strive for new things and to have the initiative to get high quality work done quickly while having proper balance with the relationships of my superiors.

I hope you read this with great enjoyment.
